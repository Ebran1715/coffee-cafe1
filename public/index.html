About Serados Cafe

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Serados Cafe brings authentic Nepali flavors to cities across Nepal. We combine traditional recipes with modern café culture.

Our Locations

We serve customers in five major cities across Nepal:

  • Bhairahawa: Siddhartha Highway, Ward No. 1
  • Kathmandu: Thamel, Kathmandu
  • Pokhara: Lakeside Road, Pokhara
  • Mustang: Jomsom, Mustang
  • Butwal: Tilottama Road, Butwal

Our Commitment

We use locally sourced ingredients and support Nepali farmers. Every cup of coffee and every dish tells a story of our beautiful country.
